In a culture where people are defined by their place of employment, J. Dayne Girard has a hard time answering the question, “So what do you do?” Some know him as their academic coach. Those familiar with his victory over three chronic illnesses see him as a wealth of information on health, nutrition, and diet. Clients and employees of Discernment Group International know him as an insightful economic writer, industry forecaster, and consultant. The Prices knew him as a caregiver. Diverse as they are, J. Dayne sees all these activities as the fruit of one singular passion: glorifying God in service to others. One of his favorite quotes is perhaps a fitting explanation of how he desires to pattern his life:

“No man has a right to be idle.[...] where is it that in such a world as this, health, and leisure, and affluence, may not find some ignorance to instruct, some wrong to redress, some want to supply, some misery to alleviate?” - William Wilberforce.

In his own words, “There is too much misery in the world to alleviate, too many needs to meet, too much error to correct, and truth to defend to not seek an ever greater capacity for service. I hope my life clearly shows that it was not the success, knowledge, skill, or experience itself that I sought as the end, but rather that these were a mere means to the end.”

RESUME:

Education: In addition to his BSBA in Finance, J. Dayne has received education in various capacities from the Duke MBA program, Yale Entrepreneurial Institute, Acton Foundation for Entrepreneurial Excellence, Expositor's Seminary, and Mises Academy.

Experience: J. Dayne has worked in the business and education fields for more than 6 years in a variety of capacities. In that time, he has worked for fortune 200 companies, millionaires, and business startups. He has instructed and trained hundreds of students, written hundreds of pages of educational material and curriculum for various venues, and has appeared on radio, all while conquering three chronic illnesses.

RENAISSERVE?:

Renaisserve is a word coined from the combination of renaissance and service. A renaissance man is someone who excels in a wide range of fields. Renaisserve is the concept of harnessing a collage of skills and abilities for greater service. Specifically, it is employing motivation, entrepreneurship, nutrition, education, economics, lifestyle design, time-management, and innovation for the service of others. And so it is that this site is dedicated to helping individuals maximize their capacity to serve others through pursuing excellence in these many areas.
